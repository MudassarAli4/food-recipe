Chickpea
Curry

Prepare: 1 hr 45 min

INGREDIENTS

2 cups canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed
2 tablespoons oil
2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
3 large tomatoes, chopped
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon ground coriander
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1 teaspoon fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
Salt to taste

Steps to Follow

Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat.
Add the chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
Add the chopped tomatoes to the pan and cook until they soften and start to break down.
Sprinkle in the ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, and salt. Stir well.
Cook the spice mixture for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
Add the drained chickpeas to the pan and stir to coat them evenly with the spice mixture.
Pour in a little water to create a gravy and let the curry simmer for 10-15 minutes.
Adjust the seasoning if needed and cook until the curry thickens to your desired consistency.
Serve hot with rice or naan, garnished with fresh cilantro if desired.
