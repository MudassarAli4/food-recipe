Chicken Karhai

Prepare : 45 min

INGREDIENTS

1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
3 tablespoons oil or ghee
3 tomatoes, chopped
2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
2-3 green chilies, chopped
1 teaspoon red chili powder
1 teaspoon turmeric powder
Salt to taste
Fresh coriander and sliced ginger for garnish

Steps to Follow

In a karahi (wok), heat oil/ghee.
Add chicken and fry until it changes color.
Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a couple of minutes.
Add chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften and oil separates.
Cover and cook on low heat until the chicken is tender and the oil surfaces.
Add green chilies and cook for another 5 minutes.
Cover the karahi with a lid and let the chicken cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is tender and the oil starts to separate from the gravy.
Once the chicken is cooked, add the chopped green chilies to the karahi. Cook for an additional 5 minutes to infuse the flavor.
Taste the karahi and adjust the salt or spices as needed. Cook for another 2-3 minutes if needed.
: Remove the karahi from heat. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and sliced ginger. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
