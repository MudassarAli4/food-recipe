Fettuccini Alfredo

Prepare : 45 min

INGREDIENTS

24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
¾ pint heavy cream
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon chopped parsley (optional)
Grated Parmesan for garnish

Steps to Follow

Boil fettuccine in salted water according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat.
Add minced garlic to the melted butter and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant, but not browned.
Slowly pour in the heavy cream, stirring constantly, and bring it to a gentle simmer.
Add salt and black pepper to the sauce, adjusting to taste.
Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese, and continue stirring until the sauce thickens and becomes smooth.
Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce, tossing to coat the pasta evenly with the Alfredo sauce.
Let the pasta simmer in the sauce for 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
Sprinkle with chopped parsley and additional grated Parmesan cheese.
Plate the Fettuccine Alfredo hot and serve immediately. Enjoy with garlic bread or a side salad.
