Butter
Chicken

Prepare: 1 hr 45 min

INGREDIENTS

1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
1/2 cup yogurt
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 teaspoons garam masala
2 teaspoons red chili powder
4 tablespoons butter
4 large tomatoes, pureed
1 teaspoon fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi
Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps to Follow

Marinate the chicken in yogurt, garam masala, and salt. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a large pan over medium heat.
Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook until it's browned on all sides. Remove and set aside.
In the same pan, add the remaining butter and saut√© the chopped onion until golden brown.
Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
Add the tomato puree to the pan and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
Return the browned chicken to the pan and stir well to coat with the sauce.
Add salt to taste and let the chicken simmer for 10-15 minutes until fully cooked.
Stir in the cream and cook for an additional 5 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.
Garnish with a sprinkle of garam masala and serve hot with naan or rice.
