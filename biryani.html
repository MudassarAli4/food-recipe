Chicken
Biryani

Prepare: 30 - 45 min

INGREDIENTS

Whole Chicken 2kg
Rice 3.5 cups
5 bay leaves
1 Tomato thinly sliced
Red chilli powder 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Turmeric powder ½ tsp
Garam Masala 1 tsp
Green cardamom 3 pieces

Steps to Follow

Add ⅓ cup oil with 2 bay leaves and black cumin ½ tsp in a pan
After 5 minutes add chicken then Add spices
Add 1 tsp ginger paste and 1 tsp garlic paste and 1 tsp ginger paste
Add Yougurt 5 tsp
Add tomato pury 5 tsp and fried onion 4 tsp
Add 1 pinch food color
Boil 16 cups water
Add 2 tsp salt , 3 bay leaves 2 green cardamom and oil 1 tsp
Add rice in water
let it boil for 4-5 minutes then After 5 minutes stain the rice
Add sliced tomato in the chicken and 2 tsp lemon juice and Add rice in chicken
