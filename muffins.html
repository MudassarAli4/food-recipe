Muffins

Prepare: 25 - 35 min

INGREDIENTS

1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
¾ cup granulated sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
¼ teaspoon salt
½ cup unsalted butter, melted
1 large egg
¾ cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
½ cup chocolate chips or fruit

Steps to Follow

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
In another bowl, whisk together the melted butter, egg, milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.
Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Avoid overmixing.
If using chocolate chips or fruit, fold them into the batter gently.
Evenly distribute the batter among the muffin cups, filling each about 2/3 full.
Place the muffin tin in the preheated oven and bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Remove the muffins from the oven and let them cool in the tin for 5 minutes.
Carefully transfer the muffins to a wire cooling rack to cool completely.
Once cooled, serve the muffins as a delicious breakfast or snack, and enjoy!
