Matar Paneer

Prepare : 30-45 min

INGREDIENTS

200g paneer, cubed
1 cup green peas (matar), fresh or frozen
1 cup green peas (matar), fresh or frozen
1 onion, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, pureed
1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 teaspoon garam masala
Salt to taste

Steps to Follow

In a pan, heat the oil or ghee over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
A Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
Stir in garam masala and salt. Cook the masala for another 2 minutes.
Add the green peas (matar) and cook for 5-7 minutes until they are tender.
Add a little water to adjust the gravy consistency. Cover and let it simmer for 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
Taste and adjust the salt or spices if needed. Cook for another 2 minutes.
Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.
