Donuts

Prepare : 45 min

INGREDIENTS

2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
½ cup sugar
2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast
½ teaspoon salt
¾ cup warm milk
2 tablespoons melted butter
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Oil for frying

Steps to Follow

Mix warm milk, 1 tablespoon sugar, and yeast. Let it sit until frothy.
Combine flour, remaining sugar, and salt. Add butter, egg, vanilla, and yeast mixture. Mix until dough forms.
Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until smooth
Let the dough rise for 1 hour until doubled.
Roll out dough to ½ inch thickness and cut out donut shapes.
Let the donuts rise for 30 minutes.
Heat oil to 350°F (175°C).
Fry donuts for 1-2 minutes per side until golden brown.
Remove and drain on paper towels.
Glaze or sugar coat, then serve.
