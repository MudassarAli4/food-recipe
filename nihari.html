Chicken
Nihari

Prepare: 1 hr 45 min

INGREDIENTS

1 kg beef shank or mutton
½ cup oil or ghee
2 large onions, sliced
2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
6-8 cups water
Salt to taste
Nihari Spice Mix:
1 teaspoon cloves
2-3 dried red chilies

Steps to Follow

In a large pot, heat oil/ghee and saut√© onions until golden brown.
Add the beef shank, ginger-garlic paste, and fry until the meat is browned.
Mix in Nihari masala and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Pour in water, bring to a boil, then cover and simmer on low heat for 4-6 hours until the meat is tender.
Mix wheat flour with water to form a smooth slurry.
Slowly add the slurry to the pot, stirring continuously until the gravy thickens.
Let the Nihari simmer for another 30 minutes, adjusting salt as needed.
Put some sliced onion and stir well
Garnish with fresh coriander, sliced ginger, green chilies, and a squeeze of lemon.
Serve hot with naan or steamed rice.
